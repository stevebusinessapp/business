How to Generate Reports
  1. Select the type of report you want to generate
  2. Provide a descriptive title for your report
  3. Set the date range for the report period
  4. Click "Generate Report" to create your report
Income Statement

Shows your business's financial performance over a specific period:

  • Total Expenses
  • Net Income/Loss

  • Total Liabilities
  • Owner's Equity
  • Financial Ratios
