What will be synced?
  • Invoices: Paid invoices will be imported as income transactions
  • Receipts: All receipts will be imported as income transactions
  • Job Orders: Completed job orders with costs will be imported
  • Waybills: Delivered waybills with charges will be imported
  • Expenses: Manual expense entries will be imported
Important Notes
  • Only transactions that haven't been synced before will be imported
  • Each transaction will be linked to its source with a reference ID
  • You can run this sync multiple times safely
  • Synced transactions will appear in your accounting dashboard
